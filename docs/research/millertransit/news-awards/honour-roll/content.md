# [H1] Miller Transit Honour Roll

[SECTION: section .entry-content]
[P] The Honour Roll program was developed to recognize employees who have demonstrated excellence throughout the year. The following criteria must be met to earn a place on the Honour Roll:
[LI] No more than 3 unexcused absences
[LI] No late reports
[LI] No Preventable Accidents
[LI] No Preventable Incidents or Onboard Injuries
[LI] Employed the entire calendar year with the only exception being their allowed Vacation Weeks.
[LI] No substantiated complaints
[LI] No Random Check Violations
[LI] Did not fail to report for any scheduled Mandatory Training
[P] All employees who earn a place on the Honour Roll are rewarded with a gift card.
