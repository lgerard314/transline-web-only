# [H1] Miller Transit Safety Awards

[SECTION: section .entry-content]
[P] In addition to receiving an Annual Safe Driving Award, in 2016, Miller Transit introduced an improved Safe Driving Award Program. This program has been put into place to acknowledge operators for achieving a milestone (i.e. 5, 10, 15, etc.) year of safe and accident-free driving. The Bus Operator will be acknowledged at the beginning of each calendar year for the previous accumulated years of safe driving. Each recognized Operator will be provided with a letter of acknowledgment; certificate of excellence; custom-designed jewelry quality Miller Transit Safe Driving pin with milestone year engraved; and a brochure which allows them to choose a gift of their liking.
