# [H1] York Region Customer Service Appreciation Award Program (CSAP)

[SECTION: section .entry-content]
[P] York Region Transit hosts an annual Customer Service Appreciation Program (CSAP) Awards luncheon to recognize:
[P] -Contractor employees who have received customer commendations;
[LI] Diamond Award (30 + Compliments)
[LI] Double Platinum Award (20-29 Compliments)
[LI] Gold Award (5-9 Compliments)
[LI] Silver Award (3-4 Compliments)
[P] -Contractors with the least number of accidents;
[P] -Most improved accident record from the past year.
