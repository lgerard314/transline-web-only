# [H1] Careers at Miller Transit

[SECTION: main #main .row]

[SECTION: section .entry-content]
[P] Miller Transit is a diversified Canadian company headquartered in Richmond Hill, Ontario. Miller Transit provides the public sector with transit and maintenance services under contract with York Region Transit.
[P] At Miller Transit, our employees matter to us. We care about establishing a workplace where employees are respected and are dedicated to providing customer service with the highest safety standards in the industry.
[P] Interested in applying? All positions available to external applicants are advertised on the Current Opportunities section. Continue to check our website for a current list of opportunities offered.
[P] We thank all applicants for their interest but only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
[P] Miller Transit is committed to a diverse and inclusive workplace. We encourage applications from all qualified applicants. An accommodation process is in place and provides accommodation for candidates with disabilities throughout the recruitment process. If you require a specific accommodation because of a disability, please advise us when you submit your application.
[P] Transit Operator (Drivers) Recruitment
[P] Transit Operators are expected to drive with a high level of skill and safety, providing courteous, professional customer service. Successful candidates possess safe driving behavior and a commitment to customer service excellence.
