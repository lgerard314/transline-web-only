## [H2] Compost and Soils
[P] Compost is a soil enhancer that should be added to your gardens yearly to provide the proper organic matter and nutrients to support the healthy growth of your plantings.
[P] Miller Compost manufactures leaf and yard waste compost that is derived from municipal curbside brown bag pick up.
[P] The difference between the standard compost and the premium compost is the particle size. Standard compost is screened through a ½” screen and the premium compost is screened through a ¼” screen.
[P] The best compost to use in your gardens would be premium compost. It has a very fine texture and is easy to till into your soils.
[P] When building a garden, you will require a structured soil compost blend. Our Ultra Soil will provide the required nutrients and structure for the support of the plants when planting.
[P] We suggest you use a sand compost blend. Our topdressing is manufactured with premium compost and a coarse washed sand that should be applied after aeration or used for an over seeding application.
## [H2] Mulches
[P] Miller Compost uses a food grade dye to colour the mulches. This dye is environmentally friendly. It is not harmful to you, your pets or the plants.
[P] MSDS – IMPACT BLACK 1.0
[P] MSDS – IMPACT DARK BROWN
[P] MSDS – IMPACT RUBY RED
[P] Mulches are good for weed suppression and water retention. Mulch helps to conserve water while keeping soil cool on the hot days of summer.
[P] The mulch will fade in colour over time. We recommend you cultivate your garden throughout the season and top up mulch as required.
[P] Our Saw Mill Series is composed of natural wood and the wood chips are collected from recycled lumber.
