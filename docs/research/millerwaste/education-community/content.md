## [H2] Truck Demonstrations
[P] Miller Waste helps inspire the future leaders of waste management with appearances at community and school-led events.
[P] At Touch-a-Truck demonstrations, we give people of all ages the opportunity to personally explore our waste collection trucks in a safe, fun, and interactive environment. Our drivers and loaders explain the truck’s unique features and give little ones a boost into the driver’s seat.
[P] We also visit schools to give curious minds a deeper understanding of the integral components of waste management. We provide tours of our trucks, demonstrate how they lift and compact waste, and introduce the principles of the circular economy.

## [H2] Community Clean-Ups
[P] Miller Waste regularly contributes to community-led clean-up events by providing waste collection bins and helping pick up litter from our common spaces. Not only do these efforts keep our communities safe and clean, but they give us the chance to divert re-usable material from the solid waste stream.

## [H2] The Princess Margaret Road Hockey To Conquer Cancer
[P] Since 2015, Miller Waste has raised funds for and competed in the world's largest road hockey fundraiser - a daylong event where rookies, veterans, celebrities and NHL heroes unite to raise funds for life-saving cancer research.
[P] The Princess Margaret Cancer Centre is one of the world’s leading cancer research and treatment centres and, since its inception, the has raised more than $35 million.
[P] We can't wait to compete in the tournament on October 3, 2026!
[LINK] Learn More → https://pmcfroadhockey.ca/
