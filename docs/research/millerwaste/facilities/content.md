### [H3] Pickering, Ontario
[P] 1220 Squires Beach Rd. Pickering, ON, L1W 3S3
[LINK] tel:+1 (905) 426-4222 → tel:+19054264222
#### [H4] Hours of Operation for Non-Hazardous Waste Depot:
[P] Monday to Friday: 7AM – 6PM
[P] Saturday: 7AM – 4PM
[P] Sunday: Closed
#### [H4] Hours of Operation for HOUSEHOLD HAZARDOUS WASTE (HHW) Depot:
[P] Tuesday to Saturday: 8AM – 4PM
[P] Sunday and Monday: Closed
[P] Minimum waste charge: $10 (60 kg and under)
[P] Blue Box material: $5.99 per load, plus tax
[P] Waste: $165 per metric tonne, plus tax
[P] Shingles: $165 per metric tonne, plus tax
[P] Electronics: Free
[P] Scrap Metal: Free
[P] Total price for all goods is subject to monthly fluctuating fuel charge.
#### [H4] Acceptable Materials and Fees for HOUSEHOLD HAZARDOUS WASTE (HHW):
[P] Free for residents of Durham Region. Daily maximum of 50 litres or 25 cans.
[P] Please keep material in its original container or in a well-marked container and ensure it is securely sealed to prevent spills.
[P] Note that vehicles must be weighed upon entry to the facility. Please drive to the scale upon your arrival.
