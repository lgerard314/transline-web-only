# [H1] Miller Waste Announces Strategic Sale of Organics and Renewable Energy Assets
[P] By Miller Waste Systems
[P] Ontario – March 23, 2026 – Miller Waste Systems (“Miller Waste”) announced the sale of its Escarpment Renewables and Maryland Farms facilities, along with certain organics-related assets in Pickering, to Walker Industries (“Walker”).The transaction was signed on March 23, 2026.
[P] This strategic decision positions these renewable energy and organics-processing assets with an organization focused on advancing renewable energy solutions, while allowing Miller Waste to sharpen its focus on integrated waste management and diversion services across its core markets.
[P] The Miller Waste employees associated with the Escarpment and Maryland facilities will transition to Walker Industries. Employees associated with the Pickering organics assets will remain with Miller Waste.
[P] “This transaction places these organics-processing assets with Walker, ensuring continued long-term success of these operations for all stakeholders. It also positions Miller Waste to remain focused on our core integrated waste and diversion services,” said Joe Johnson, President, Miller Waste Systems. “We value our decades-long, strong relationship with Walker and are confident this transition provides resilient continuity for our employees, customers, and communities.”
[P] Miller remains committed to organics diversion, sustainability and environmental stewardship and will continue its working relationship with Walker to ensure responsible organics processing.
[P] Customers, suppliers, and stakeholders should expect seamless continuity in service and operations.
#### [H4] About Miller Waste Systems
[P] Miller Waste Systems is a 100% Canadian-owned, privately-owned company with 65 years of experience in waste collection, material recovery, transfer station operations, composting, and diversion services. Serving communities across Ontario, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, and Manitoba, Miller remains committed to safety, sustainability, and delivering dependable service to municipalities and businesses. For more information, visit www.millerwaste.ca.
