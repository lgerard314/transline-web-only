# [H1] Grow Your Career at Miller Waste
[P] By Miller Waste
[P] With over 1,200 collection vehicles and more than 2,000 dedicated employees across Canada, Miller Waste Systems is more than just a waste collection company. We’re a sustainable waste management organization striving for a cleaner, greener planet offering municipalities, businesses, industries, and institutions sustainable disposal solutions. When you work for us, you’re not just an employee, you’re part of a team dedicated to making an impact. Curious about what it’s like to be a part of Miller Waste Systems? Let’s dive in.
[IMG] photos/0749d8b892b1__NJB_2088-2-2048x1367.jpg  (alt: "", natural 501×335)
[P] Three Miller Waste employees walking into work smiling.
## [H2] Advance Your Learning
[P] Education is power and learning new, advanced ways to do something is always something we strive to achieve. Whether you’re just starting or have years of experience, Miller Waste Systems provides training beyond the basics.
[P] We focus on educating our employees by providing them with access to specialized skill-building programs, including courses in operating large waste collection vehicles and understanding how to properly handle all types of waste. We ensure our teams are well-equipped so that they can reach their full potential.
[P] “I’m in the Hillsdale department, working the ASL (Automated Side Loader) for the county. I’ve been here since November 2021, and I love the work and the four-day schedule. I plan to stay for the 8–9-year county contract and hope we hold onto it longer than that.”
[P] Miller Waste Systems promotes a culture of growth from within. We support every step of your journey, from student co-op opportunities and new graduate placements to positions for seasoned professionals. Each day offers dynamic work and meaningful responsibilities, helping you to advance your skills and reach your full potential.
[IMG] photos/12b4f9f1f151__NJB_2398-2048x1367.jpg  (alt: "", natural 503×336)
[P] A photo of an employee looking down the line at the Miller Waste recycling facility.
## [H2] Contribute to Bettering the Planet
[P] As we said before, we are not a garbage company but a waste management organization. With our second core value being commitment, we do not waver in our responsibility to our customers, communities, and environment. At Miller Waste Systems, you’ll be part of a team actively working towards environmental sustainability and helping reshape the future of waste management. Through our facilities, such as our Escarpment Renewables plant, which transforms your organic waste into renewable energy and an organic alternative to traditional fertilizer, we show that we are serious about taking the next steps to protect our environment.
[P] “Love the job. Great place to work and a great group of people. Wouldn’t change careers for the world.”
[IMG] photos/5a1791b63339__NJB_2488-2-2048x1367.webp  (alt: "miller waste employee infront of truck", natural 284×24)
[P] A Miller Waste employee standing in front of a Miller Waste garbage truck smiling.
## [H2] A Resilient Industry
[P] Economic downturns can create challenges across industries, but at Miller Waste Systems, we are proud to be part of a long-standing industry. Waste management is an essential service that continues to thrive regardless of market conditions which means job security for our staff.  We’re constantly evolving and expanding, with ample opportunities for career growth as we keep communities clean and green.
[P] “I just started working as a picker for this company in Nova Scotia, and I love it. You really feel like you’re part of the team, and I’m excited to grow here, hoping to eventually work up to a driver position.”
[P] When you join Miller Waste Systems, you become part of a community dedicated to positive environmental change. A career here isn’t just about a job, it’s about continuous growth, impact, and a long-term commitment to bettering our world. If you’re interested in building a career with us, visit our website to see available positions.
