### [H3] Our Services
### [H3] Front-End Loading
[P] Choose the bin type and collection frequency that best meets your needs. Miller Waste offers sturdy steel bins and plastic toters to accommodate the waste and recycling generated by your site's staff, customers, and tenants.
### [H3] Roll-Off Services
[P] Consider roll-off bins if you don't need waste collected very frequently, or if you need a container for a short-term project, such as construction and demolition projects, interior and exterior renovations, clean-ups, moves, and landscaping projects.
### [H3] Compactors
[P] Rent or lease-to-own self-contained compactors to maximize space in your container, discourage pest and scavenger activity, and reduce odour.
