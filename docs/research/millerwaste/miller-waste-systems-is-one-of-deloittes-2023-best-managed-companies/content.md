# [H1] Miller Waste Systems is one of Deloitte’s 2023 Best Managed Companies
[P] By Miller Waste
[P] Miller is proud to once again have been named one of the best managed companies by Deloitte. This is the 13th consecutive year Miller has been included in the list, which is the nation’s longest-standing initiative to recognize leading privately-owned Canadian companies. As such, Miller maintains its membership in the Platinum Club, a small network of winners who have retained their Best Managed status for seven years or more.
[P] The Deloitte’s best managed list honours overall business performance and sustained growth and recognizes the efforts of the entire organization in reaching excellence.
[P] This achievement reflects Miller’s commitment to “Only the Best”: we are dedicated to providing the best, most sustainable waste management solutions, bringing out the best in our people, and finding the best ways to collaborate with the communities we serve.
[P] Pictured below are members of the Miller team at the winners’ Symposium and Gala on May 10, 2023 at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre.
