# [H1] Miller Waste Partners with Operation Litter Ridder and City of Vaughan for Earth Day Clean Up
[P] By Miller Waste
[P] Miller Waste was proud to partner with the City of Vaughan to sponsor Operation Litter Ridder’s Earth Day clean up event on Saturday, April 23.
[P] A dedicated group of volunteers braved the rain to conduct the first plastic bag free clean up in Woodbridge, Ontario.
[P] By day’s end, they had collected 480 kilograms of trash, including discarded coffee cups, shoes, tires, hubcaps, and even a fire hose! In other words, they prevented over 1000 pounds of litter from making its way to our home, businesses, schools, fields, and waterways.
[P] Miller was happy to supply the event with a 40-yard tarped dumpster bin and 12 toter bins, which we used to haul away the trash for proper disposal.
[P] Congratulations team, for making such a large impact on Earth Day!
