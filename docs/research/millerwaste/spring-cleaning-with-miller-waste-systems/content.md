# [H1] Spring Cleaning with Miller Waste Systems
[P] By Miller Waste
[P] Spring is in full bloom, meaning seasonal cleaning may be top of mind! It’s the time of year to freshen and renew our homes and sometimes that means revisiting the daunting clutter build up you’ve put off from last year. Check out our list of spring-cleaning tips and tasks and you’ll be ready to get started in no time!
## [H2] 1. Prepare Your Equipment and Supplies
[P] Ensure you’re well stocked up on supplies such as your sprays, soaps, sponges, and dusters and make sure your equipment like vacuums and mops are ready to use. If you run out of a necessary product, it can disrupt your day and make it less likely that you’ll finish the job.
## [H2] 2. Make a Schedule
[P] Scope out your home – inside and out. Ask yourself what areas need the most work. Do you have rooms that you often skip over and tell yourself “That mess is for another day”? Those are the best places to start. Start by jotting down which room you’ll tackle on each day of the week to help manage the larger project at hand.
## [H2] 3. Organize items into three distinct piles: keep, donate, or throw away. Consider renting a temporary dumpster bin to easily dispose of the items you’re ready to toss.
[P] Miller’s roll-off services are ideal for clean-up projects: we’ll deliver the right size bin directly to your driveway and haul away your junk when you’re done. The best part? You can place your order online in minutes. Just choose your size and let us know when we should drop the bin off and pick it up.
[P] Miller’s commitment to landfill diversion means we will sort your material and recover as much of it as possible. We’ll sort your junk, remove contaminates, and send recyclable material to our secondary processing facilities where it will be re-processed to its highest end use.
