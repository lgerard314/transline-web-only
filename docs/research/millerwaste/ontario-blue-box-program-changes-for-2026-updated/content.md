## [H2] What is Extended Producer Responsibility?
[P] Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) refers to the Canadian legislation that transfers full responsibility for managing printed paper and packaging material to producers and first distributors of the material.
## [H2] FAQ
[LINK] What is the new EPR Blue Box program? → 
[P] The new EPR Blue Box program shifts the responsibility and cost of recycling packaging from municipalities to the producers who place that packaging on the market. For commercial businesses, this may bring changes depending on your municipality or sector; some businesses may see new reporting or sorting requirements under producer responsibility. If you’re unsure whether your business is affected, contact Miller Waste and we will help you understand how EPR applies in your area.
