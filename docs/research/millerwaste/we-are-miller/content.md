# [H1] The Miller Difference
[P] Taking care of our communities, customers, our people, and the environment.
### [H3] Miller Waste specializes in sustainable waste management solutions that divert recoverable material from the solid waste stream.
[P] With a robust fleet of 1200 collection vehicles and more than 2000 employees across Canada, Miller offers sustainable waste management solutions to municipalities, businesses, industries, and institutions. We specialize in waste collection, dumpster bin rentals, and the separation and treatment of recycables and organic waste.

## [H2] Our History
[P] Miller Waste Systems’ roots in waste management date back to the early 1960s, when the company began providing waste management services to the Town of Markham. Since then, Miller Waste has grown from a two-truck operation to a diverse waste management company that, in addition to providing waste collection services to businesses and municipalities, specializes in the design, construction, and operation of material recovery and organic waste processing facilities. We are inspired by the work ethic of our founding family, who own and operate the business to this day.
[IMG] photos/c41fcc609117__NJB_3985@2x.jpg  (alt: "", natural 16×16)
## [H2] Our Commitment to the Environment
[P] Miller Waste acknowledges our responsibility to the environment. As such, we focus on diverting re-usable material from the landfill and transforming it into new, useful products and sources of renewable heat and energy.

# [H1] Tribute To Leo
#### [H4] We remember Leo Anthony McArthur, who will forever be associated with Miller Waste.
[P] Leo was a giant in the industry and among the first rank of his generation’s entrepreneurs. Over the course of 40 years, Leo and his business partner, John Carrick, expanded the reach and scope of Miller’s groups of companies across Canada and the United States.
[P] Leo’s career was characterized by hard work, integrity, and above all, a passionate commitment to all of Miller Waste Systems’ employees.
[P] Leo was deeply involved in local community and industry affairs and his charitable, industrious spirit lives on.
