#### [H4] Where to Bring Hazardous Waste

[SECTION: section .elementor-section.elementor-top-section]
[IMG] photos/8c05a5e1b1d8__Haz-Waste.webp  (alt: "", natural 360×217)
[P] Unfortunately, we cannot accept Household Hazardous Waste (HHW) at this location (this includes anything that is corrosive, flammable, explosive, or poisonous, including:
[P] batteries, asbestos, paint, solvents, primers, stains, thinners, ammonia-based cleaners and disinfectants, windshield wiper fluid, oil filters, motor oil, brake fluid, and more.
[P] Please dispose of your hazardous waste at:
[P] Miller Waste Pickering Transfer Station
[P] 1220 Squires Beach Road Pickering, ON, L1W 3S3
[P] Free for residents of Durham Region. Daily maximum of 50 litres or 25 cans.
[P] Please keep material in its original container or in a well-marked container and ensure it is securely sealed to prevent spills.
