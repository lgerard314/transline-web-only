## [H2] Port Perry Transfer Station Discount Terms and Conditions

[SECTION: section .elementor-section.elementor-top-section]

[SECTION: section .elementor-section.elementor-top-section]
[P] 1. Eligibility
[P] a) This discount is available to customers who provide their email address via the form on https://millerwaste.ca/port-perry-waste-drop-off-depot/.
[P] b) This discount is only available to customers who do not have an account with the Miller Waste Port Perry Transfer Station.
[P] 2. Redemption
[P] After entering their email address, customers will receive a discount code via email. The customer must present this code to a staff member each time he or she unloads waste material at the Port Perry transfer station. With the code, the customer will save 10% on the cost of their load before tax and fuel.
[P] The discount will apply to each load/visit. It is only redeemable for loads dropped at the Miller Waste Port Perry Transfer Station.
[P] 3. Duration and Termination
[P] a) This promotion will run from Monday, May 11, 2026 until 5:00 PM EST on Monday, August 31, 2026.
[P] b) This promotion may be modified or discontinued at any time without notice.
[P] c) All decisions made by Miller Waste regarding code validation and redemption are final.
[P] 4. Privacy:
[P] a) The information collected will only be used by Miller Waste for internal tracking and customer engagement and outreach purposes.
[P] b) Personal information will not be shared or sold to third parties.
[P] By participating in this program, customers agree to these terms and conditions.
