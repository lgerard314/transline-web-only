# [H1] Miller Waste Systems to Launch New Waste Collection Contract in Windsor, Ontario on April 1, 2025
[P] By Miller Waste
[P] Windsor, ON – Miller Waste Systems is proud to announce the start of its new residential waste collection contract with the City of Windsor, beginning April 1, 2025. This milestone displays the confidence that the City of Windsor has in Miller Waste’s continued support of municipalities across Ontario and underscores the company’s commitment to supporting local communities and delivering dependable, environmentally responsible waste management services.
[IMG] photos/747e4fe80f9c__Miller-Truck-Sunrise-Logo-Updated-2-1-1.webp  (alt: "Miller waste truck at sunrise", natural 212×24)
[P] As part of this new contract, over 40 new jobs have been created in Windsor across operations, maintenance, and support roles—bringing long-term employment opportunities and economic benefits to the region.
[P] “Miller Waste is excited to become embedded in the Windsor community,” said Joe Johnson, President of Miller Waste Systems. “Miller Waste is a Canadian company; 100% Canadian. We’re a proudly Canadian, family-owned company that believes in investing in the communities we serve. The addition of new local jobs reflects our long-term commitment to Windsor, and we look forward to delivering safe, reliable, and respectful service to residents.”
[IMG] photos/fdb845f4f189__NJB_0287-2-2048x1367.webp  (alt: "", natural 562×375)
[P] In business since 1961, Miller Waste Systems is Ontario’s leading waste management and diversion company, serving more than 2.1 million households weekly across the province. With over 60 years of experience, over 2,300 employees, and over 40 locations—including transfer stations, material recovery facilities, organics processing sites, and composting sites turning waste into energy—Miller Waste is focused on service delivery and supporting the environment.
[P] Miller Waste has also been recognized as one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies for 14 consecutive years—a reflection of the company’s excellence in leadership, strategy, culture, and innovation.
[IMG] photos/fff972fe68ca__BestManaged_Platinum.png  (alt: "Canada's best managed companies platinum member", natural 396×24)
[P] The Windsor contract includes curbside collection of garbage, yard waste, and organics for residential households. Miller Waste brings a strong reputation for operational excellence, customer-first service, and a sustainability-focused approach that helps communities reduce landfill waste and recover valuable resources.
[P] “As we begin operations in Windsor, we’re not just bringing trucks—we’re bringing a culture of safety, accountability, customer service, and community support,” added Johnson. “We’re here to build partnerships, create local impact, and provide the high-quality service Windsor residents deserve. That’s what the Miller Waste diamond represents.”
## [H2] About Miller Waste Systems
[P] Founded in 1961, Miller Waste Systems is a 100% Canadian-owned company delivering integrated waste management and diversion solutions to municipal, commercial, and industrial clients. With a strong focus on environmental sustainability, innovation, and customer service, Miller Waste helps communities thrive while diverting waste from landfills and generating renewable resources through advanced composting and energy recovery programs.
[P] For more information, visit www.millerwaste.ca
