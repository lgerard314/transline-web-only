# [H1] Miller Waste Systems Named One of Deloitte’s Best Managed Companies in 2024
[P] By Liana Crocco
[P] Miller Waste is proud to once again be included in Deloitte’s list of the best managed companies in Canada.
[P] This is the 14th consecutive year Miller has been recognized by the program, which highlights Canadian companies that demonstrate industry-leading performance, global business practices, and sustained organic growth.
[P] As such, Miller maintains its membership in the Platinum Club, an exclusive network of winners who have retained their Best Managed status for seven years or more.
[P] This year’s winners have also been hailed by Deloitte for combining strategic acuity, bold leadership, and consistent adaptability to drive success.
[P] A family-owned business since 1961, Miller Waste’s service offerings have expanded beyond waste collection to include a full suite of waste management solutions. For the last twenty years, we have specialized in the design, construction, and operation of Material Recovery Facilities and organic processing plants, where we divert re-usable material from the solid waste stream and help convert it into new and useful products.
[P] Over sixty years, Miller has grown from a two-truck operation to a national waste management company serving tens of thousands of customers in multiple provinces. Despite our size and standing in the industry, we operate with the same friendly, collaborative spirit on which our company was founded and remain committed to providing businesses, communities, and industries with unmatched service when it comes to reliability, consistency, and regard for health, safety, and wellbeing.
