## [H2] The TransLine49° Advantage
## [H2] At TransLine49°, we help with the transboundary movement of waste from the United States into Canada. We offer access to unique disposal and recycling options that mitigate your US liabilities. Firstly, US Superfund legislation holds no jurisdiction in Canada, providing relief from regulatory burden. Additionally, we ensure direct liability protection backed by the Manitoba Provincial government.
