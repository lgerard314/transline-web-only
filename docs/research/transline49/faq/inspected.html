Frequently Asked Questions

Who is TransLine49° Environmental Services?

TransLine49° is a US company headquartered in St. Louis, MO and is affiliated with Miller Environmental Corporation, a Canadian-based company providing an alternative option for final disposal and recycling of waste in North America.

What are the services you provide?

TransLine49° assists with cross-border hazardous waste permitting as well as scheduling of the logistical movement of material. We provide access to a Canadian owned and operated final disposal/recycling facility located at Manitoba, Canada.

What are your Disposal and Recycling Capabilities?

Miller Environmental Corporation, which receives these materials, is authorized to accept industrial waste with a primary focus on reusing, repurposing, and recycling.

Which certifications do you hold?

TransLine49°’s affiliated company, Miller Environmental Corporation is ISO 9001, 14001, 45001 certified and a CHWMEG, Inc. audited Canadian facility, demonstrating their commitment to quality, environmental and occupational health and safety standards.

What are your safety measures in doing these projects?

TransLine49° and Miller Environmental Corporation have adopted a behavior based safety program, making all employees responsible for each others’ safety. This encourages the safety of the general public along with the property at all workplaces.

Every task includes a risk assessment to find and eliminate workplace dangers.

  • Workplaces are inspected regularly.
  • Every occurrence, including near misses is recorded, and followed up.
  • Ongoing enhancement quantified by prearranged audits.
  • A dedication to proactive leadership and assistance that will ensure appropriate instruction and tools are available to enable staff to carry out their duties.
Which materials do you accept?

Including but not limited to:

  • Acids, alkalines and other corrosive wastes.
  • Flammable organic liquids, solids and debris (Hand Sanitizer).
  • Oxidizers and reactives.
  • Adhesives, resins & paints.
  • Toxic materials (Nicotine and pharmaceuticals).
  • Chemical lab packs and household
    hazardous waste.
  • PFAS/PFOS (Per-and polyfluoroalkyl).
  • Catalyst waste.
  • Contaminated soils.
  • Waste waters
How do customers request a quote for available services?

TransLine49° values your time and satisfaction. We guarantee to turn around a customer’s waste profile in 48 hours or less, and get these treated as quickly as possible.
