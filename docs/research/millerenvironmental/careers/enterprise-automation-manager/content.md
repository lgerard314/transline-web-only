## [H2] Current Career Opportunities
[IMG] photos/39e5eb9caf48__logo-miller-outlined-2026-300.webp  (alt: "", natural 156×155)
[P] Committed to Leadership in Our Industry
[P] Enterprise Automation Manager
[LI] Serve as a strategic partner to business leaders, offering problem-solving, mentorship, and collaboration to help shape IT strategies. Maintain pulse on emerging technologies, service provider options, and monitor competitor activity to guide innovation.
[LI] Establish and oversee long-term technology plans, ensuring that technology is effectively leveraged to achieve business objectives.
[LI] Promote the organization’s adoption of automation technologies to build competitive advantage and enhance customer experience, providing guidance and support to business leaders on how to accomplish this.
[LI] Lead major system modernization initiatives, transitioning legacy back-office systems to modern platforms that support automation objectives.
[LI] Direct both strategic and day-to-day technology operations with a strong focus on delivering excellence, optimizing the use of financial and human resources.
[LI] Engage proactively with internal stakeholders and external clients to identify needs and deliver innovative technology solutions.
[LI] Collaborate with technology leaders to influence the development of practices, processes, methodologies, and standards that are oriented towards both customer satisfaction and technological advancement.
[LI] Design, purchase, or adapt technology solutions and processes that provide clear competitive advantages for business units or the broader organization.
[LI] Recruit, retain, and develop talent, fostering a culture of high performance and teamwork that consistently delivers value and positively shapes the broader Technology team environment.
[LI] Manage risk and ensure compliance in alignment with Canadian and international governance and standards, and data protection requirements.
[LI] Postsecondary education in Information Systems, Computer Science, Business, or a closely related discipline. 10+ years’ experience as a technology leader, with a proven track record in either a business unit or a technology-focused role.
[LI] 5+ years of direct customer-facing technology experience, expertise in automation, and progressive experience supporting ERP or other enterprise business systems.
[LI] Advanced analytical, problem-solving, and systems design capabilities.
[LI] Strong verbal and written communication skills and interpersonal skills required to effectively build relationships and communicate with business leaders, internal stakeholders, and customers.
[LI] Demonstrated success navigating complex, matrixed environments and effectively influencing teams and stakeholders without direct authority.
[LI] Responsible for designing, purchasing, or adapting technology solutions and processes that deliver a distinct competitive edge to individual business units or enhance the broader organization.
[LI] Proven leader with outstanding delivery expertise, strong communication skills, and a talent for building trust and fostering effective collaboration across teams and functions.
[LI] Ability to collaborate alongside technology leaders to drive the advancement of practices, processes, methodologies, and standards, ensuring alignment with both customer needs and technological priorities.
[LI] Extended Health Care Benefits including dental care and prescription drug benefits.
[LI] Travel Insurance coverage
[LI] Annual Cost of Living Allowance increases
[LI] Emphasis on career growth and advancement of employees.

[LI] Safe work culture driven by employee participation.
