## [H2] Tracking
[P] Miller utilizes a modified U.S. based waste tracking database that allows Miller to track and follow each step in the management of waste from a customer’s site. This system allows for cradle to grave tracking of all waste and reports can be generated to accommodate a customer’s needs as required.
