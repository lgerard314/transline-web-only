## [H2] Overview
[P] Miller Environmental provides industrial cleaning services to all industry sectors ranging from confined space cleaning of underground and above ground holding tanks, high pressurized steam cleaning of equipment and piping, sludge pits, and bulk liquid/gondola rail car cleaning.

## [H2] Miller Environmental provides...
[P] Industrial cleaning services to all industry sectors. We operate a fleet of vacuum trucks and pressure washer units to clean tanks and other industrial equipment.
