# Benefits & Rewards — `/careers/benefits-rewards/`

## Hero
> Joining Miller means joining a company that prioritizes the growth and development of all its employees.

## Company Investment Statement
Miller recognizes that organizational strength derives from dedicated personnel and invests in expanding team knowledge through:

- Annual cost of living allowance increases
- Career growth and advancement emphasis
- Extended Health Care Benefits (dental, prescription drugs)
- Matching pension plan
- Travel insurance coverage

## Support & Development Programs
- Annual vacation allocation
- Diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Employee Referral program
- Comprehensive safety training
- Incentive programs
- Personal days off
- Personal Protective Equipment allowance program
- Post-Secondary Education Awards ($1,000 annually for dependent students)
- Safe work culture through employee participation
