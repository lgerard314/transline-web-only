# Industrial Waste Treatment — `/industrial-services/industrial-waste-treatment/`

## Hero
- **Headline:** Industrial Waste Treatment
- **Lead:** Miller Environmental operates the licensed Vaughn Bullough Environmental Centre (VBEC) on 160 acres, equipped to receive large volumes of waste in multiple forms.

## Overview
Miller's facility provides unique benefits through "the ability to treat industrial organic and inorganic waste both in liquid and solid forms at one location." The site accepts containers from pails to bulk truck loads and profiles all incoming waste to determine recycling or repurposing opportunities.

## Treatment Processes Offered
- Inorganic waste processing
- Aqueous waste processing
- Liquid organic waste processing
- Solid organics processing
- Recycling

## Treatment and Processing Capabilities

### Specialty Soil, Sludges & Solids Treatment
Miller formulates chemistries for contaminated soils and hazardous solids, with exclusive capability to receive materials "24/7 to mitigate major spills and environmental incidents immediately."

Treatable materials:
- Glycol contamination
- Heavy metal impacted materials
- Acid or alkali contamination
- Toxic/environmentally hazardous substances
- PFAS and PFOS impacted material
- Flammable solids
- Chlorinated soil
- Catalysts

### Inorganic Waste Processing
- Stabilization of metals
- Wet or dry inorganic sludge solidification & disposal
- High-value metal salts recycling capability
- Fluorescent tube shredding, stabilization & solidification
- Chemical processing for inorganic chemicals
- Stabilization of processed chemical residues

### Liquid Organic Waste Processing
- Sludge/water separation
- Solvent/fuel blending (waste to energy)
- Latex paint bulking & solidification
- Oil-based paint bulking/fuel blending
- Oil filtering and sludge separation

### Solid Organic Waste Processing
- Solidification and disposal of oily debris
- Dissolution of solid materials including pharmaceuticals
- Aerosol processing

### Special Waste Processing
- Flammable solids
- Spent catalyst recycling and/or disposal
- Industrial wastewater treatment
- Hydrovac sludge/slurry
- Chlorate neutralization

### Miscellaneous Processing & Recycling
- Industrial plastic shredding, washing, and recycling
- Lead acid and alkaline battery recycling
- Solvent recycling
- Electronic waste recycling
- Industrial container cleaning and tote recertification

### Special Processing Projects
Development of specialized chemistries for unique waste streams through SR&ED Canadian Federal Government initiative.
