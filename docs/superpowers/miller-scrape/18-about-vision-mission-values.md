# Vision, Mission, and Core Values — `/about-us/vision-mission-and-core-values/`

## Vision
> To be recognized as a trusted provider of sustainable environmental waste and process solutions.

## Mission
> To provide innovative and accountable environmental management solutions that advance an environmentally sustainable world.

## Core Values

### Respect
> We will treat all fellow team members, the community, our customers, our business partners and the environment with courtesy, dignity and respect.

### Empowerment & Accountability
> We take personal responsibility for excellence and quality in everything we do and hold ourselves accountable as professionals at all times.

### Teamwork
> We rely on each other for our successes, and we encourage diversity of culture, gender and skill set to enable a positive team environment.

### Healthy Work Environment
> We are committed to providing all team members with a positive, fun and safe work environment.
