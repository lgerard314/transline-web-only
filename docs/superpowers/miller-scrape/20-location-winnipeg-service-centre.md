## Facility Overview
The site functions as "a hub for the coordination and movement of inbound/outbound waste headed to its final disposal location" and maintains licensure as a **PCB storage facility**. The centre houses the Customer Service Technicians and Projects/Field Services Team.

## Services at this Location
- Customer Waste Collection
- Emergency Response
- Industrial Cleaning
- Industrial Waste Treatment
- Project Management
- Specialty Recycling
- Stewardship
- Vacuum Truck services
- Field coordination and project services
