# Job Posting — Plant Manager — `/careers/plant-manager/`

- **Title:** Plant Manager
- **Location:** Winnipeg, MB (1803 Hekla Ave, Winnipeg, MB R2R 0K3)
- **Reports to:** not specified

## Role Summary
Lead plant operations at one of Western Canada's largest independent waste treatment facilities. Responsible for *"the design, administration, and advancement of Miller Environmental's ERP ecosystem"* while managing safety, people, processes, and financial performance in a dynamic industrial environment.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead zero-incident safety culture and ensure regulatory compliance
- Direct plant teams through supervisors and build accountability culture
- Drive process optimization and operational scalability
- Manage budgets (OPEX) and lead cost control initiatives
- Develop capital investment (CAPEX) business cases
- Integrate operations across Safety, Maintenance, Engineering, and Commercial teams

## Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in Chemical, Mechanical, Environmental, or Industrial Engineering
- 10+ years in industrial/plant operations
- Experience with chemical processing or hazardous waste (preferred)
- Safety leadership background
- Financial acumen and hands-on leadership style

## Compensation & Benefits
"Competitive salary and benefits package" — extended health care, travel insurance, pension matching, annual cost-of-living increases.

## How to apply
Email résumé to **hr@millerenvironmental.mb.ca**.
