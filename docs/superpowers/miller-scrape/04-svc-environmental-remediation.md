## Our Process

1. **Assessment & Planning** — Site inspections, Phase II ESA reviews, and Remedial Action Plan (RAP) preparation.
2. **Containment & Safety** — Barriers, berms, air monitoring, and regulatory coordination.
3. **Specialized Excavation** — Hydro vac soft-dig near utilities, deep excavation for heavy metals contamination, selective demolition.
4. **Licensed Treatment & Disposal** — Contaminated material processed at our licensed hazardous waste treatment facility.
5. **Verification & Sign-Off** — Independent lab testing, third-party oversight, and final regulatory closure.
