## Affiliations & Memberships

1. **Manitoba Environmental Industries Association (MEIA)** — The MEIA works to advance environmental and clean technology opportunities in Manitoba through networking, education and support for its members.
2. **Manitoba Chamber of Commerce** — Represents all businesses with a commitment to champion sustainable economic development and foster a business-friendly climate.
3. **Canadian Manufacturers & Exporters (CME)** — Has been advocating for and representing member interests to help businesses grow.
4. **Construction Safety Association of Manitoba** — A non-profit organization run by and for the building construction industry in Manitoba.
5. **Commitment To Opportunity, Diversity & Equity (CODE)** — A Chamber community movement dedicated to fostering inclusive workplaces.
6. **Manitoba Trucking Association** — Develops and maintains a safe and healthy business environment for its industry members.
7. **Winnipeg Chamber of Commerce** — Committed to building a city where industry prosperity and community wellness grow.
8. **Ontario Waste Management Association (OWMA)** — The voice of the waste management sector; provides research and expertise.
9. **Miller Waste Systems** — Offers waste management solutions in collection, haulage, disposal, and organics.
10. **TransLine49° Environmental Services** — Environmental service company under White Owl Family Office Group ownership.
