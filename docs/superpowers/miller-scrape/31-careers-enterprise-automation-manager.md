# Job Posting — Enterprise Automation Manager — `/careers/enterprise-automation-manager/`

- **Title:** Enterprise Automation Manager
- **Location:** Winnipeg, MB (1803 Hekla Ave, Winnipeg, MB R2R 0K3)
- **Reports to:** not specified

## Role Summary
Strategic technology leadership position responsible for designing and advancing Miller Environmental's ERP ecosystem and its evolution into an *"AI-enabled enterprise environment."* The role bridges business and technology functions to modernize operations and enhance decision-making.

## Key Responsibilities
- Act as strategic partner to business leaders on technology strategy and innovation
- Establish long-term technology plans aligned with business objectives
- Lead system modernization initiatives transitioning legacy systems to modern platforms
- Direct strategic and day-to-day technology operations
- Engage stakeholders to identify needs and deliver technology solutions
- Recruit, develop, and retain talent
- Manage risk and ensure compliance with governance standards

## Requirements
- Postsecondary education in Information Systems, Computer Science, Business, or related
- 10+ years technology leadership experience
- 5+ years direct customer-facing technology experience with ERP expertise
- Advanced analytical and systems design capabilities
- Strong verbal/written communication
- Experience navigating complex, matrixed environments
- Proven ability to influence without direct authority

## Compensation & Benefits
"Competitive salary and benefits package" — extended health care, travel insurance, cost-of-living increases, pension matching, vacation, personal days, EAP, safety training, PPE allowance, education awards.
