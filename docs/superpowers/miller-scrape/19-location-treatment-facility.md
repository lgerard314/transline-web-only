# Treatment Facility (VBEC) — `/treatment-facility/`

## Facility Name
**Vaughn Bullough Environmental Centre (VBEC)** — A fully licensed hazardous waste treatment facility positioned 70 kilometres south of Winnipeg, utilizing 64 hectares in the rural municipality of Montcalm.

## Capabilities
- Engineered Lined Landfill
- Clay Base Lined Processing Cells
- Specialized Organic/Inorganic Processing
- Decontamination Facility
- Plastic Shredding/Recycling
- Batch Water Treatment
- Hydro Vac Slurry Receiving
