# Licencing Information — `/about-us/licencing-information/`

## Overview
Transportation and handling of hazardous waste requires special licensing under **Manitoba's Hazardous Waste Regulation (M.R. 195/2015)**.

The regulatory framework establishes "a system of controlling hazardous waste from 'cradle to grave'" and involves registration of generators plus licensing of carriers and receivers.

The facility license specifies "administrative and technical conditions under which waste must be managed" while ensuring safe, ethical, and environmentally responsible operations.
