# Health & Safety — `/about-us/health-safety/`

## Hero / Lede
Safety is Miller's top priority and primary core value, demonstrated by their Total Recordable Incident Rating (TRIR). The company's safety program aims to protect all stakeholders — employees, clients, and community members.

## Safety Management System
In April 2018, Miller implemented a behaviour-based safety management system requiring "continual hazard-risk assessment process to be used by anyone performing work." The system engages the entire workforce to eliminate unsafe behaviours.

## Training As A Value
Miller maintains extensive training programs tailored to each employee's job duties and responsibilities across all organizational positions.

## New Worker Program
The **Green Hand Program** identifies and supervises employees with fewer than 90 days of experience to prevent injury and environmental harm. This program ensures new workers understand their occupational health and safety rights, reporting procedures, and associated hazards.

## Emergency Response Preparedness
Miller prioritizes employee, contractor, visitor, and community health and safety through the **Incident Command System (ICS)**. The company completed company-wide ICS training in 2021 and implements a "scenario based training plan" to practice emergency responses and reinforce continuous improvement.
